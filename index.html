About Me




Martin Brown was born in Northern Ireland but has lived in North Wales since 2004. After completing a PhD in Biochemistry, he worked briefly in industry until training to be a teacher in 1998, preferring people rather than test tubes. He is highly experienced at teaching in the UK education system, including all Sciences from Key Stage 2 through to Advanced level and International Baccalaureate, as well as Mathematics to Key Stage 3.

He is an Examiner for both the Edexcel and WJEC examination boards and has 20 years’ experience as an Ofqual Inspector for the Science Curriculum. Alongside his full-time work as a Science teacher, Martin has worked for Oxford Study Courses since 2010, providing group tuition in Chemistry for the International Baccalaureate Diploma in Oxford, Cambridge, Munich, and Singapore. He has also been offering private tuition in the Sciences since 2005, with many excellent results. He holds a current DBS certificate.

In his spare time, Martin is an aviation enthusiast, flying virtual planes all over the world from the comfort of his own desk. He is also an avid fly fisherman and nature lover who likes nothing better than a long hike through the hills – especially if there is a river at the end of it.
